✈️ Telegram Integration

Connect Telegram to log transactions via chat and get daily balance updates.

This requires hosting a small bot (this app runs entirely in your browser, so it can't receive messages on its own):
1. Message @BotFather on Telegram → /newbot → copy the bot token.
2. Message your new bot, then visit api.telegram.org/bot<TOKEN>/getUpdates to find your chat ID.
3. Host a script (e.g. on a Raspberry Pi, Cloudflare Workers or a £3/mo VPS) that receives commands and reads a synced export of your JSON backup.

Supported commands: /balance /spent /add [amount] [category] [description] /bills /goals
